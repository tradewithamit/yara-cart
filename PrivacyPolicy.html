Privacy Policy

At Yara Cart, we value your trust and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you shop with us.

1. Information We Collect

- Personal details like your name, phone number, email, and shipping address.
- Payment details (processed securely via third-party gateways).
- Order history and browsing behavior to improve your shopping experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

- To process and deliver your orders.
- To provide customer support.
- To share updates, offers, and promotions (only if you opt-in).
- To improve our website and services.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your details may only be shared with trusted service providers like payment gateways and delivery partners, solely for fulfilling your order.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to keep your data safe. However, no method of online transmission is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage or disable cookies from your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us. You can also opt out of promotional communications anytime.
